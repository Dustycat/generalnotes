
# Deep Desert

# Checklist for Building a Base
1. Key Dates
	1. Tear down base Monday night (PT)
	2. Build base Tuesday after 4am (PT)
2. Large Storage should contain the following at a minimum
	1. Construction tool
	2. 100 metal, 300 granite
	3. Steel Ingot x10, Cobalt Paste x100, Calibrated Servok x30. Supports up to 5 pentashields
	4. Copper Ingot x100. Support 4 water cistern
	5. Iron Ingot x210. Supports 4 chest (80 iron), repair station (40 iron), Supports Improved Blood Purifier (50 iron), Repair Station (40 iron)
	6. Copper Ingot x30. Supports Recycler.
	7. Silicon Block  x16. Supports Improved Blood Purifier
	8. Complex Machinery x20. Supports Improved Blood Purifier
3. Check water level on self
	1. Most likely, there will be sun while building the base, so you will need water
	2. Wear stillsuit if possible
4. Fuel up carrier, repair, take storage, and fly to DD
5. Build Advance Subfief
	1. Make it on a flat surface
	2. Give permission to everyone in guild
6. Build foundation from high ground to low ground
7. Build walls, power (at least 8?), and pentashield
8. Secondary Priority
	1. Build storage containers
	2. Improved blood purifier and 4 water cistern
	3. Fabricator
9. Third Priority
	1. Repair station
	2. Recycler
